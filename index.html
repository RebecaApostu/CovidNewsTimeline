Covid-19 - Official News Timeline (Card View) versus Cancelled Bookings (Blue Top Bars)

This timeline features official news related to Covid-19 in five selected countries. The blue bar charts displays accommodation booking cancellations recorded per the same timeframe. The bars display the number of cancellations per day, if any. The cancellations are obtained from the Booking Activity Dataset - filtering both by status 'canceled' and last modified date in order to obtain the time stamp when they have occured.
